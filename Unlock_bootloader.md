# Unlock Bootloader — Samsung A02 (SM-A022F/DS)

> **Warning:** Proceed at your own risk. **All data will be wiped.**

## Step 1

- Enable `OEM Unlocking` in Developer Options
- Boot into Download Mode by pressing (Volume Up + Down) and plug into USB
- Long press Volume Up to unlock bootloader — **all data will be wiped**

## Step 2

- Do the phone setup
- Connect to Wi-Fi
- Don't need Samsung or Google Account
- Finish the setup
- Wait around 15 minutes
